Get to Know Us

Our founder Jack Frost, a long time resident of Anytown, USA, started Arctic Treats in 2020 with a simple mission: to bring joy and refreshment to the community through delicious snow cones. What began as a small stand at local events has blossomed into a beloved neighborhood fixture, known for our friendly service and mouth-watering flavors. We are passionate about creating memorable experiences for our customers, whether it's a family outing or a quick treat on a hot day. At Arctic Treats, we believe that every snow cone is more than just a dessert; it's a moment of happiness shared with friends and family. Join us at our two convenient locations celebrating the simple pleasures of life, one snow cone at a time!
